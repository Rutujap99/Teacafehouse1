About us

Our Purpose

In India Tea is not just a hot piping drink, its an emotion, gossip, conversation,bond beginning and a lot more. With the growing number of tea rooms in India, it's needless to say that the kind of growth and business opportunity it brings on the table is exceptional. The concept of tea house is to bless the chai lovers with the best of tea flavours & comfort to every soul by the taste of brewed magic, be it regular, masala or premium . Create Better... May it be a nice treat after dinner, or you're feeling under the weather and need something soothing, or just looking to start the day fresh & invigorated, here at T.House, we want to continue growing the love for tea & create better.
